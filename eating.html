Healthy Eating Habits

Discover the importance of eating healthy with these food groups!

Fruits

Fruits are packed with vitamins. Try apples, bananas, and berries!

Vegetables

Vegetables give you strength. Carrots, broccoli, and peas are great choices!

Grains

Whole grains like rice and oats provide long-lasting energy!

Proteins

Proteins help you grow strong. Enjoy eggs, beans, and fish!

Dairy

Dairy keeps your bones healthy. Try milk, cheese, and yogurt!

Healthy Snacks

Enjoy healthy snacks like nuts, seeds, and dried fruits to stay energized between meals.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay refreshed and healthy!
