Sleep Tips for a Healthier You

Good sleep is important for growing minds and bodies! Follow these tips for better rest:

Set a Bedtime

Go to bed at the same time every night to help your body stay on schedule.

Limit Screen Time

Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime. Try reading a book instead!

Keep Your Room Dark

A dark room helps you sleep better. Use blackout curtains or turn off the lights.

Morning Sunshine

Get some sunlight in the morning to help your body wake up naturally.
